At United Drilling Tools Limited (UDTL), we remain deeply committed to integrating 
Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles into our business strategy. We 
acknowledge the challenges posed by air emissions, water scarcity, waste management, 
workplace safety, and evolving stakeholder expectations. To address these, we have set clear 
and measurable targets, including reducing air emissions through advanced technologies, 
maximizing water recycling, ensuring 100% eco-friendly waste disposal, enhancing employee 
well-being, and expanding our CSR initiatives in education, healthcare, medical aid to 
underprivileged individuals, and community infrastructure development.

the proportion of treated water reused in our plants, strengthened workplace safety through 
proactive training and monitoring, and expanded our community outreach programs, resulting 
in improved educational opportunities and healthcare access for local communities. We 
also enhanced governance frameworks to ensure transparency, ethical conduct, and active 
stakeholder engagement. While we take pride in these accomplishments, we remain committed 
to continuous improvement, innovation, and responsible growth that delivers long-term value 
for all stakeholders.
