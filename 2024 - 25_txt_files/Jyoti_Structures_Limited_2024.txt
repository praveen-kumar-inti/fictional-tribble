Incorporated in 1974, JSL is an Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) contracting 
company in the power transmission and distribution networks across India and global markets. 
JSL has broad experience in executing projects across the entire T&D value chain.  
 
The Company has three lines of operations which include transmission lines, substations and
