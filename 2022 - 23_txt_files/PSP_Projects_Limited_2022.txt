The company installed RCS-Rail Climbing System at the project sites for fall protection (The RCS is a safe

secure connection between the climbing unit and the building, ensuring additional safety in windy conditions. 
The modular design allows for versatility in various climbing applications and can be easily adapted to suit job 
site-specific requirements. The system is also easy to move using a crane or mobile climbing hydraulics, adding
